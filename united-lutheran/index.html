Making the Good News Visible

United Lutheran is an ELCA congregation on the north side of Racine, Wisconsin. This community exists to make the good news of Jesus visible through relevant welcoming worship services, creation of communities of inclusion and learning, and a commitment to advocacy and justice.

Following in the Reformation tradition Martin Luther began over 500 years ago, we will continue to love God and love our neighbor as we seek justice for all of creation.

United Lutheran Church exists to joyfully welcome all people to make the good news of Jesus Christ visible. We gather around story and meal on Sunday mornings at 10:45am. You are welcome to be curious about what it means to be Christian, Lutheran, or join us so we can serve the neighborhood together.
